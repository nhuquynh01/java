About us

Steak is a dish of Western origin, characterized by a thin and flat piece of beef tenderloin, perpendicular to the muscle fibers and processed by grilling or lightly frying, combined with specialized spices. such as oil, fat, onion. This dish has become quite familiar in the modern culinary style of Vietnamese people. And when it comes to the place to enjoy European standard steak in Hanoi, no one is unaware of the name Rico Steakhouse. Nearly 30 years of experience in the profession, Rico Steakhouse has become a brand that leaves an indelible mark in the hearts of Hanoi diners. The restaurant's dishes are full of flavor, rustic, with their own distinctive style thanks to the talented and skillful hands of experienced chefs. In particular, the traditional steak - a combination of Western steak and typical culinary flavors of Hanoians has been researched, built and developed by Rico Steakhouse over the past 30 years. From 1988 until now, Rico Steakhouse has become familiar to not only the people of the capital but also the address that many customers from far away come to. Despite owning a close and airy design space, the restaurant does not focus too much on form, but the most important impression to "score" with diners is the rich and attractive menu. In addition to the main steak dishes such as traditional steak, New York steak, American beef steak, some other outstanding dishes of the restaurant will also make it hard for you to forget even once enjoyed.
